Abhilash Chandra Singh

I am a Transportation Engineering Doctoral Candidate at Imperial College London, U.K., advised by Aruna Sivakumar currently working on endogeneity and choice set issues, especially their applications to residential location choice models with a large number of choice alternatives.

Prior to my current position at Imperial, I worked at the Center for Transportation Research, while obtaining a Master of Science in Engineering with a specialization in Transportation Engineering [2018] at The University of Texas at Austin, U.S.A. I also earned a Bachelor of Technology in Civil Engineering [2016] from Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay.I have received Department Dixon Scholarship and Wellcome Trust Project Scholarship for Ph.D. in UK, Texas District Student Fellowship [2017-2018] and Graduate fellowship to undertake graduate studies at UT Austin [2016-2018].
